Lisa D'Amato, America's Next Top Model All-Stars Winner, Injures Face in &quot;Strange Freak Accident&quot;

Lisa D’Amato has found the “silver lining” in the recent “freak accident” that seriously injured her face — she’s been told by doctors she’ll look even better post-plastic surgery! The America’s Next Top Model All-Stars winner, 31, worried her fans on Twitter Jan. 19 after she posted a picture of her bloody face from the hospital.

PHOTOS: America’s Next Top Model All-Stars contestants

“This is real. I broke my nose last night folks!” she announced with the photo. “Got rushed to the plastic surgeon and now I’m finished. Who wants to hang out? Lol JK. But seriously, I’m fine . . . I need to chill on my gymnastics. I’ll be back to normal in a month. I hope I like my new nose!”

D’Amato broke her nose and injured her forehead and lip while goofing around on the Colorado set of the upcoming indie film, Cowboys and Indians.

“I am very lucky that as a dancer, gymnast, and stage performer that I’ve never gotten hurt from it. With all my daring antics, I’ve been so blessed all my life when it comes to that,” she explained on Twitter. “Even though I broke my nose last night from my ‘daring shenanigans,’ I still refuse to live in fear. My face (supposedly) will look even better when it’s healed — which is a silver lining in this situation — but also, I finally got my share of injury/ pain from the universe to remind me to be more careful. I’ll be fine everyone!”

And she really is healing very nicely. D’Amato, who was named the winner of America’s Next Top Model All-Stars in December 2011, posted a makeup-free updated photo of how her face has been healing. She also apologized to fans if her previous photos scared them.
