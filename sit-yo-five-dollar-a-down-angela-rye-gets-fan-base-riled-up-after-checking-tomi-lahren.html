Sit Yo Five Dollar A Down Angela Rye Gets Fan Base Riled Up After Checking Tomi Lahren

Angela Rye had choice words for conservative pundit Tomi Lahren. (YouTube video screenshot)

Fans of political analyst Angela Rye had her back Tuesday when conservative firebrand head Tomi Lahren tried to come for the fiery CNN commentator. She was ultimately shut down, however.

Lahren, an outspoken supporter of President Donald Trump and all things right-wing, took shots at Rye over her support for the rights of undocumented immigrants. The social media tiff unfolded after Rye criticized former Arkansas Gov. Mike Huckabee after he tweeted out an outwardly racist message targeting House Minority Leader Nancy Pelosi.

“You’re racist AF,” Rye wrote of Huckabee’s post, which featured a photo of heavily-tattooed Latino gang members purporting to be members of Pelosi’s new campaign committee. “You traffic in fear by otherizing folks. Your biggest concern about border crossings is being out-numbered. It is about people who are more likely to vote for progressives than to endorse your bigotry.”
