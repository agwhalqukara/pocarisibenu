TOYA JOHNSON AND ROBERT RUSHING THROW DAUGHTER A WEDNESDAY THEMED PARTY

Toya Johnson and Robert Rushing have a five-year-old on their hands! The celebrity couple commemorated their daughter Reign Rushing’s fifth birthday with an Addams Family themed party on Wednesday.

Reign specifically wanted to celebrate Wednesday Addams for her special day. “My baby wanted a Wednesday themed birthday celebration and @dazzlemeparties understood the assignment,” Toya said of Dazzle Me Parties who was responsible for the details of the party. “@rockinjumpsmyrna was the perfect place to host @reign_beaux’s 5th birthday party,” Johnson shared regarding Rockin Jump Smyrna, which served as the bash’s venue.

“The kids and adults had a ball,” Toya exclaimed. “Thank you to everyone that came out to celebrate my baby’s birthday on a Wednesday,” the celebrity mom mused in reference to the decision to hold the party on a Wednesday. “We love you.”

Reign Rushing is Toya Johnson and Robert Rushing’s only child together. The little one made her mom a proud parent of two daughters on February 8, 2018. Reginae Carter, who is Toya’s oldest child, sent birthday wishes in Reign’s direction earlier this week.

“Happy Birthday to my little sister @reign_beaux,” Reginae wrote on her Instagram page. “This little girl means so much to me,” the young adult told her social media fans. “I can’t believe she’s 5 already,” Reginae exclaimed.

“I remember giving you your first bath. I remember not wanting to leave the hospital because I was so in love with your wittle self. Now you’re My True Mini-me,” Reginae wrote to her little sister. “Sassy as ever and you don’t play about your mama,” the young adult mused. “My lil twin. Today we celebrate you and you know we gon’ turn all the way up,” Reginae wrote to Reign on the little one’s birthday. “You’ll forever be my little baby.”

Reign Rushing is Robert Rushing’s first child. Toya and Robert celebrate one year of marriage later this year.
