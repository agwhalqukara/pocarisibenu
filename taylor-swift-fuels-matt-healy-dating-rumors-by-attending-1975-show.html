Taylor Swift Fuels Matt Healy Dating Rumors by Attending 1975 Show

Another "Blank Space" filled? Taylor Swift's latest night out with Victoria's Secret Angel besties Lily Aldridge and Karlie Kloss may be a sign that she's back in the dating game. On Thursday, Dec. 4, the "Style" singer was spotted having a grand ole time in the balcony of The 1975's show in NYC, fueling rumors that she's dating the band's frontman Matthew Healy.

Swift, 24, stood out to concertgoers while dancing with her famous BFFs. She wore an all-black ensemble and her trademark red lipstick. "I'm in love with @marhunt and @lilyaldridge," she captioned an Instagram pic that night.

Just last month, Swift attended Healy's concert in L.A. with Selena Gomez and Ellie Goulding. The English indie rocker, 25, later confessed that the two exchanged phone numbers.

"I met Taylor Swift, that was really nice," he said during a radio interview with Shazam Top 20's Angus O'Loughlin in November. "We exchanged numbers. Let's see what happens." He added: "I mean bloody hell, what am I going to do? Go out with Taylor Swift? She's a sensation, I wouldn't say no."

If she says yes, Healy will be the first guy Swift will go out with since her high-profile relationship with One Direction's Harry Styles. The friendly exes (they've since gotten back in touch) had a bad breakup in January 2013 after three months together. Swift admitted in the September issue of Rolling Stone that she hasn't been on a single date since the split.

"I don't like seeing slide shows of guys I've apparently dated. I don't like giving comedians the opportunity to make jokes about me at awards shows," she told RS. "I don't like how all these factors add up to build the pressure so high in a new relationship that it gets snuffed out before it even has a chance to start. And so I just don't date."
