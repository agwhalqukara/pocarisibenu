Gabrielle Releases 'Show Me' Music Video

Taken from her first studio album in eleven years Under My Skin, Show Me is Gabrielle’s latest single.

Show Me’s video was directed by Dan Massie at Electric Light Studio sees Gabrielle and her band performing at Walthamstow Town Hall. The simplicity of the video and the showing of the behind the scenes moments onset make it a magical watch.

The track which she worked on with Grammy-nominated producer Steve Chrisanthou is a soulful, vocally emphasised number which celebrates her comeback in the perfect way. If you want a throwback fact: the album release will mark 25 years since the release of her debut single Dreams!

Across October and November, Gabrielle will be heading out on tour with Rick Astley across the UK as a special guest. The tour will stop in London’s Eventim Apollo on 8th and 9th November.

Under My Skin is set to be released on 17th August and can be pre-ordered from here.
