David Platt Biography

David Platt was born in Atlanta, Georgia, and graduated from Georgia University with a Bachelor of Arts in journalism. He later went to New Orleans Baptist Theological Seminary. He holds several degrees, including an M.Div, a Ph.D., and a Th.M. He became Dean of Chapel at New Orleans Baptist Theological Seminary after finishing his doctorate. Platt also worked at New Orleans’ Edgewater Baptist Church. When Hurricane Katrina flooded the parsonage, he moved into a residence.
