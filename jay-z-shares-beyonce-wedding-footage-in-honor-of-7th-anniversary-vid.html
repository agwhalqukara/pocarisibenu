Jay Z Shares Beyonce Wedding Footage in Honor of 7th Anniversary: Vid

Through the years, Beyonce and Jay Z have been “dangerously,” “crazy,” and “drunk” in love, and on April 4, the couple celebrated their seven-year wedding anniversary. 

Several days later, the rapper, 45, whose real name is Sean Carter, posted wedding footage of his secret 2008 nuptials to the “Partition” singer, 33, on his official Instagram account.

The brief clip was also used as background footage during the couple’s On the Run Tour last summer. It played as they sang the cover of “Forever Young” at the end of every concert.
