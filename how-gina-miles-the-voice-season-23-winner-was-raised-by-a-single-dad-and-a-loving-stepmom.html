How Gina Miles, The Voice Season 23 Winner, Was Raised by a Single Dad and a Loving Stepmom

Who is Gina Miles?

Gina Miles is a 19-year-old pop singer-songwriter who won the 23rd season of the American talent competition The Voice, having competed on the team coached by Niall Horan. She was born on November 6, 2003, in Paxton, Illinois, and moved to Sacramento, California, with her aunt to pursue a career in the music industry. She has already released an EP titled Who Are You in 2022.

Who are Gina Miles’ parents?

Gina Miles was raised by her single dad, Ryan Galey, who worked as a disc jockey. She helped him prepare his equipment for his gigs since she was 14, which sparked her interest in music. She also has an older sister named Lucy.

Gina Miles’ biological mother is not known, but she has a stepmom named Amy Buler. Ryan and Amy have been together for 13 years and are reportedly married for 7 years. Amy supported Gina when she decided to move to Sacramento and chase her dreams.

How did Gina Miles win The Voice?

Gina Miles auditioned for The Voice with Katy Perry’s “The One That Got Away” and impressed two of the four coaches, Kelly Clarkson and Niall Horan. She chose to join Team Niall and advanced through the battles, knockouts, playoffs, and live shows with her powerful vocals and emotional performances.

She sang songs like “Skinny Love” by Bon Iver, “Somebody That I Used to Know” by Gotye feat. Kimbra, “Wicked Game” by Chris Isaak, “All I Want” by Kodaline, “Style” by Taylor Swift, and “Nothing Compares 2 U” by Sinéad O’Connor. Her finale performance of the latter song received a positive reception online and was called “a special moment on The Voice” by Niall Horan.

Gina Miles was announced as the winner of The Voice season 23 on May 23, 2023, beating Grace West from Blake Shelton’s team by a slim margin. She won $100,000 and a record deal with Republic Records.

What’s next for Gina Miles?

Gina Miles is currently working on her debut album with Republic Records and hopes to release it soon. She also plans to go on tour with Niall Horan and perform for her fans. She has expressed her gratitude to her coach, her family, and her supporters for helping her achieve her dream.

According to Hollywood Life, Gina Miles said: “I’m just so thankful for everything that has happened to me. This is just the beginning of my journey and I can’t wait to share more music with everyone.”
