Brittney Griner, Wife Glory Johnson Expecting First Child Together

First comes marriage, then comes baby! WNBA stars Brittney Griner and Glory Johnson are expecting their first child together, the couple announced on Instagram on Thursday, June 4.

“#SUCCESS #BLESSED#WelcomeToTheJohnsonGrinerFamily,” 24-year-old Johnson, who is carrying the pregnancy, captioned a “bun in the oven” cake on Instagram to announce their happy news. 

“We are excited for Glory and Brittney and wish them the best in the upcoming year,” Tulsa Shock president Steve Swetoha said in a statement via Facebook. “We look forward to seeing Glory back in a Shock unform in 2016."

The athletes, who got married last month following a nine-month engagement, used a sperm donor to conceive, TMZ reports.
