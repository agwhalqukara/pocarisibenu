KIM Kardashian has flaunted her extremely tiny waist and arms in a skintight bodysuit on Instagram.

The reality TV star sparked concern recently from fans with her major weight loss.

Kim appeared in Ciara’s Instagram video on July 19, wearing a SKIMS brown bodysuit.

Ciara was promoting her new song, Jump featuring Coast Contra, with a star-studded video.

It started out with the singer dancing and lip-syncing along to her song, then she put her hand up and the next celebrity appeared on the screen.

Along with Kim and Ciara, the video featured Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson, Vanessa Bryant, Natalia Bryant, J Balvin, Big Latto, and La La.

A Kardashian Instagram page reposted Kim’s part.

She stood in a skintight bodysuit with her hand on her hip, lip-synching the words: “You like when I make the thing (Jump!)”

Her long blonde hair was pulled back into a ponytail.

It was unclear where the Hulu star was, but a white backdrop could be seen behind her.

This video comes after fans have sparked concern for the 41-year-old after her sudden weight loss.

BEACHY KIM

Earlier this month, Kim showed off her tiny waist again along with her abs in a bikini on the beach.

She enjoyed playtime with Saint, as the two were recently seen digging through the beach sand.

The SKIMS founder slipped into a sporty two-piece with a cobalt top and white bottom.

The mother-of-four looked relaxed as she took a moment to get down on the sand with her son and built shapes in the sand.

The photos were taken during her recent family vacation to Turks and Caicos.

THINNER THAN EVER

While in New York City earlier this month, Kim looked thinner than ever as she wore a $3k Balenciaga tracksuit.

The reality star was spotted leaving her hotel wearing the super-baggy blue, black, and white tracksuit.

The TV personality nearly drowned in the look as her weight loss has continued post-Met Gala appearance in early May.

Kim paired the look with a gargantuan pair of black bug-eye sunglasses and wore her platinum blonde hair tied back in a bun.

She finished off her casual outfit with a fluffy black bag and full glam makeup.

DRAMATIC WEIGHT LOSS

Kim recently revealed that she dropped 16lbs in three weeks to fit into Marilyn Monroe’s iconic $5 million dress for the Met Gala.

The reality star, who is famous for her hourglass figure, has since shocked fans with her petite frame while attending events and on social media.

The Sun exclusively revealed that Kim’s family is concerned she’s losing too much weight and is “barely sleeping”.

FAN THEORIES

Fans have shared theories on the reasoning behind the LA native’s sudden weight loss.

Several suggested that it could be to do with Pete’s exes which include the likes of Ariana Grande, Kaia Gerber, and Pheobe Dynevor.

The user wrote on Reddit: “Kim’s weight loss – a theory.

“I could be way off here but I’ve just realized all Pete’s exes are tiny. Like, incredibly thin. Even for Hollywood. I wonder if Kim noticed too.”

Others agreed, with one writing: “Yeah he was with Ariana and that Kaia Gerber so I was like is he into Kim? Then she got skinnier.

“Which is kind of sad because it’s his looks that are trailing in the relationship, not her.”

Another added: “You could be onto something. Also – as he is so skinny, maybe she’s trying to feel ‘smaller’ to match him physically.”

A third posted: “Pete’s been with skinny white women, she probably feels insecure about not being his typical type.”

Kim revealed on The TODAY Show in June that although she lost 21 pounds to fit into the iconic dress, she does not plan on losing any more.

“I’m not trying to lose any more weight, but I have more energy than ever. I cut out so much sugar, and a lot of junk food I was eating, that I didn’t even realize it. A lot of fried foods, and I just completely changed my lifestyle,” she told the show’s hosts.
