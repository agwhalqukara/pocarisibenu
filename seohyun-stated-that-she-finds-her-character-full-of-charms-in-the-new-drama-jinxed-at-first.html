Seohyun Stated That She Finds Her Character Full Of Charms In The New Drama Jinxed At First

KBS 2TV’s upcoming drama Jinxed at First has released fresh stills of SNSD’s Seohyun. Jinxed at First is a fantasy romantic story that revolves around two characters with opposing fates. Na In-woo portrays Gong Soo-Kwang, a poor, unlucky guy who has accepted his fate. However, his life is turned upside down when he encounters the goddess of fortune, who leaps out to defend him and reverse the curse despite being locked away by a chaebol family. Seohyun characterized her character Seul-bi as “interesting” and “inquisitive,” adding that the character is full of charms, which drew her in.

Seohyun of SNSD discusses her new role in the forthcoming drama Jinxed At First.

She has been cast in the character of Seul-bi, a woman with the rare power to see the future of anybody she touches. Seul Bi is forced to live in prison due to her abilities, away from everyone’s prying eyes. Despite the harsh treatment she has received, she nevertheless exudes optimistic energy as she fantasizes about a happy existence. Her character is described as curious. Seohyun went on to say:

“She doesn’t isolate herself, and she craves the outside world.” She sees things from a different angle. She also doesn’t mind exchanging affection.”

Moreover, she said that her persona exudes warmth and enthusiasm in life, despite the harsh treatment she may get.

“She’s a character that is filled with curiosity and a desire to know all there is to know about the universe.” She is the sort to take action and is continuously craving the outside world.”

Seohyun went on to say that Seul-bi is more fascinated about the world than she is terrified of it. She’s upbeat, fearless, inquisitive, free of inhibitions, and full of charms. She went on to describe Seul-attractions bi’s in further depth, revealing:

“Since Seul Bi has lived in her own universe, her only pals have been various species and fairy tale princesses.” That is why she loves all living things and has a unique perspective on the world. She’s a real lover who has no reservations about falling in love.”

In Jinxed at First, SNSD’s Seohyun co-stars with Na In-woo.

Maknae from SNSD will star with Na In-woo in the forthcoming fantasy romance drama. Na In-woo will portray Gong Soo-Kwang, a fish trader at Seodong Market with a special curse and an unlucky bog of bad luck. He is attractive and talented in his profession, and Gong Soo-life Kwang’s has been completely altered as a result of an unexpected occurrence. Despite having accepted his bad destiny, his life is turned upside down when he encounters Seul-bi, the goddess of fortune.
