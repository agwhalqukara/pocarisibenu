Samuel Stevens Wikipedia, Age & Bio

Born on 4 July 1996, Stevens is 26 years old. His birthplace is Fort Worth, Texas, United States, and is American by nationality.

The player pursued his high school education at Kapaun Mt Carmel High School in Wichita, Kansas. As an amateur, he played golf at high school and won the 2015 Kansas Amateur Golf, and finished second in the 2017 Kansas Amateur.

After graduating from high school, he attended Oklahoma State University. There he graduated with a degree in Finance in 2018.

Samuel Stevens Parents & Family Life

Samuel was raised by American parents in Wichita, Kansas. His father is Charlie Stevens and his mother is Pam Stevens.

A third-generation golfer, his father, and grandfather were professional golfers. Charlie played on the PGA Tour and Korn Ferry Tour and was on the golf team at Oklahoma State University.


Likewise, his grandfather Johnny Stevens played in the PGA Tour in the 1960s. Both his father and grandfather have won the Kansas Amateur before him.

Stevens’ mother Pam played collegiate tennis at Oklahoma State University. He is one of the two children in the family and has a sister named Abby Stevens.

His Career Journey

Stevens played golf at Oklahoma State University from 2014 to 2018. He was a member of the Cowboys’ 2018 NCAA Championships-winning team.

In 2018, he turned professional after college and played the Fore The Kids Fall Shootout golf tournament. He played several minor tournaments until 2022 such as Colbert Charity Classic, Sand Creek Championship, and Grand Casino Open.


Samuel made his PGA Tour debut in February 2022 at Honda Classic where he made the weekend cut with 12th place and finished T55. He has played 14 PGA Tour events until 2023.

In September 2022, he earned his PGA Tour card for the 2022-23 season after finishing in a tie for 12th place in the final round of the Korn Ferry Tour Championship.
