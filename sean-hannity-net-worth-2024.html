About Sean Hannity Net Worth

Sean Hannity is an American talk show host, conservative political commentator and an author. He is well known for been the host of The Sean Hannity Show, a nationally syndicated talk radio show and has also hosted a commentary program, Hannity on Fox News, since 2009. Hannity worked as a general contractor and volunteered as a talk show host at UC Santa Barbara in 1989. He later joined WVNN in Athens, Alabama and shortly afterward, he went to join WGST in Atlanta.

Sean Hannity Biography

Sean Patrick Hannity was born on 30 December,1961 in New York City. He is the son of Lillian who worked as a stenographer and a corrections officer at a county jail and Hugh Hannity who was a World War II veteran and family-court officer. He was the youngest of four siblings and the only boy. All his grandparents immigrated to the United States from Ireland. In his youth, Hannity worked as a paperboy delivering issues of the New York Daily News and the Long Island Daily Press. Hannity attended Sacred Heart Seminary in Hempstead, New York and St. Pius X Preparatory Seminary in Uniondale, New York. He continued at New York University and Adelphi University, but did not graduate from any of them.
