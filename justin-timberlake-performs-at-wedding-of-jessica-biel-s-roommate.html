Justin Timberlake Performs at Wedding of Jessica Biel's Roommate

Love wins! Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel attended the wedding of one of Biel’s close friends in Norwalk, Conn., on Saturday, June 27. Though the event had been planned prior to last week’s big announcement, the Supreme Court of the United States’ ruling legalizing same-sex marriage made it that much sweeter as Dana Panzer and Amy Wicker tied the knot.

Biel, 33, who was roommates at Tufts University with Panzer, danced up a storm at the soiree. One eyewitness said, “She looked amazing. You would never know she just had a baby because she is slim and trim."

Biel and her hubby, 34, left baby Silas, 2 months, at home, but the Grammy-winning star did offer up an extra special wedding gift for the newlyweds. Sitting down at a keyboard, the former boy bander rocked out to his hit “Señorita.”
