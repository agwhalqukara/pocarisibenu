Video: WWE Reveals New World Tag Team Championships to Replaces WWE Raw Tag Titles | News, Scores, H

Tim Nwachukwu/Getty Images

On Monday, WWE announced that the Raw Tag-Titles will now be known as the World Tag-Team Championships to go along with brand new title belts.

The duo of R-Truth and The Miz, known as Awesome Truth, were officially crowned as the World Tag Team Champions after they emerged victorious alongside A-Down Town Under following a six-way tag team ladder match during the first night of WrestleMania 40 on April 6.

The two belts were presented to them by Triple H himself during Monday's Raw.
