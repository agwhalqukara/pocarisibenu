Anthony Alfredo Girlfriend, Family Life &amp; Ethnicity Facts

Nicknamed “Fast Pasta”, Anthony Alfredo had a career transition from eSports to real life sport. He decided to dedicate his career in racing after spending nearly half a decade in the online gaming platform.

Anthony Alfredo is an American car racer who competes full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series. He drives the No. 38 Ford Mustang for Front Row Motorsports.

Anthony Alfredo Wiki: Family Life & Ethnicity

Born as Anthony F. Alfredo on 31 March 1999, he is of age 22 now. He is the youngest child of parents Rob Alfredo and Veronica Alfredo. His father is the source of inspiration in his life, who is a self-made man from the extremely hard work and dedication.


The racer has an elder brother named Robbie and a sister named Brianna Alfredo. He grew up with his siblings in Ridgefield, Connecticut, United States.


When it comes to ethnicity, Alfredo is of Italian descent. His mother is Italian, and he loves Italian dishes, especially Pasta.

His Career And Net Worth

Earlier, Anthony won the 2016 Southeast Limited Late Model Series Pro Division championship.

In 2017, he was selected to drive for Dale Earnhardt, Jr.’s JR Motorsports (JRM) team in the Late Model division of CARS Tour.

Moreover, he competed full-time in the NASCAR K&N Pro Series East in 2018. Likewise, he competed in the NASCAR Gander Outdoors Truck Series with DGR-Crosley for thirteen races in 2019.


In 2021, Alfredo announced that he would compete full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series with Front Row Motorsports.

Anthony net worth is contributed by his earnings from his racing career. Furthermore, he generates exceptional revenues from his merchandise products so far. His actual net worth figure is under review.
