David Tennant - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Married, Facts

David Tennant happens to be a famous Scottish actor, television personality, and social media personality. David Tennant is famous for his roles as the tenth incarnation of The Doctor in the BBC sci-fi series Doctor Who (2005–2010), and Giacomo Casanova in the BBC comedy-drama serial Casanova (2005). Similarly, the Scottish actor has gained a huge fan following on his social media accounts.

Early Life

David Tennant was born in the year 1971 and celebrates his birthday on the 18th of April every year. Likewise, as of now, the Scottish actor is 52 years old. He was born in Bathgate, Scotland. Similarly, the actor belongs of Scottish nationality and was born under the star sign Aries.

Moving on to the family background of the Scottish celebrity, he was born to Alexander “Sandy” McDonald and Helen McDonald. Likewise, his father happens to be a minister who previously served as the Moderator of the General Assembly of the Church of Scotland. Talking of siblings, he has a brother and sister named Blair and Karen. Tennant informed his parents when he was three years old that he wanted to be an actor because he was a fan of Doctor Who, but they tried to persuade him to pursue more conventional work.

Education

Talking about the educational background of the Scottish actor, he first went on to attend Ralston Primary and Paisley Grammar School. While at the school, he regularly appeared in School plays as well. Likewise, he was later accepted into the Royal Scottish Academy of Music and Drama.

Career and Professional Life

David Tennant happens to be a prominent Scottish actor and television personality. Likewise, Edith MacArthur, a Scottish actress, recognized his talent. She predicted that he would become a successful stage performer after attending his first performance when he was 11 years old. He made his professional acting debut while still in high school. His debut television appearance was in an episode of the British drama ‘Dramarama. Likewise, His first major part was in the British TV series ‘Takin’ Over the Asylum. It was set in a Glasgow psychiatric hospital and revolved around a hospital radio station.

Moreover, His efforts with the Royal Shakespeare Company gained him significant recognition. His performance in the 1996 production of ‘As You Like It’ was well-received. Likewise, His first leading part in cinema was in Mika Kaurismäki’s romantic comedy-drama film ‘L.A. Without a Map’, which he directed in 1998. His next major film role was in the 2005 fantasy picture “Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire,” the fourth episode of the popular Harry Potter film series. He played Barty Crouch Jr., a death eater. The film was a big commercial success as well as a multi-award winner.

Awards

David Tennant has won a slew of honors over his career. He won multiple prizes for his portrayal in ‘Doctor Who,’ including the National Television Awards for ‘Outstanding Performance’ (2006) and ‘Most Popular Actor’ (2007), as well as the BAFTA Cymru Award for ‘Best Actor (2007).
