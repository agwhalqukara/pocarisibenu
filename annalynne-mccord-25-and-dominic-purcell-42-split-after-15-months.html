AnnaLynne McCord, 25, and Dominic Purcell, 42, Split After 15 Months

It was fun while it lasted.

Fifteen months after Dominic Purcell, 42, was first spotted kissing AnnaLynne McCord, 25, on the set of 90210 in L.A., the couple has called it quits, multiple sources tell Us Weekly. The stars met while shooting Officer Down in early 2011; the film has yet to be released.

One insider believes McCord decided to end the relationship after her two sisters, Angel and Rachel, both got engaged. "Though AnnaLynne's not in a rush to get married, her sisters getting engaged must have made her evaluate her own relationship to some degree," the insider tells Us.

Adding that Purcell seemed "more into AnnaLynne than she was into him," the insider says the starlet "recognized the imbalance in their romantic dynamic."
